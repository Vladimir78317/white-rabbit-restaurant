White Rabbit v2.7 — Flexible shifts and automatic table distribution

Added:
- Administrator can still create a scheduled waiter shift.
- Waiter can open a walk-in shift without prior scheduling, from 09:00 to 23:00.
- Walk-in shift immediately appears in the administrator shift list.
- Tables are automatically and evenly distributed between all waiters with OPEN shifts.
- When a waiter opens or closes a shift, table assignments are recalculated.
- Administrator can run redistribution manually with the new button.
- Shift list shows whether a shift is scheduled or walk-in.
